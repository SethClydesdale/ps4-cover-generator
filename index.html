Create Your Cover Image

This tool allows you to create your very own personalized Cover Image for use on your PSN Profile. Use the Toolbox at the bottom of your screen to start creating your Cover Image. For the highest quality Cover Image, press to go into fullscreen mode.

Any changes you make in the toolbox will be reflected in the preview below. When you're finished editing your Cover Image, scroll to the bottom of the page to generate it. Tip : You can close the toolbox to get a better view by clicking its black handle.
