Affiliation and Information

"PS4 Cover Generator" is not affiliated with nor a product of Sony Interactive Entertainment Inc.

"PS4 Cover Generator" is an Open Source tool used for Creating and Generating Cover Images for PlayStation Network acccounts. It was created by Seth Clydesdale and is maintained by the Github community.

Images and/or Logos

PS4 Cover Generator offers an assortment of graphical resources to its users that have been modified for ease-of-use on the PlayStation 4 Web Browser. These resources were acquired through various websites and are not created by PS4 Cover Generator. Credit for these resources goes to the original author(s).

Removal

If you are dissatisfied with PS4 Cover Generator using any of the graphical resources you created, please contact us via one of the links in the "Contact Info" section of the footer. We'll ask for verification that you're the original author and respect your decision to remove your content.
