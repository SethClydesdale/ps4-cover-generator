Create Your Cover Image

Welcome to PS4 Cover Generator! This tool allows you to create your very own personalized Cover Image for your PlayStation Network account directly in the PS4 web browser or on your PC. Use the Toolbox at the bottom of your screen to start creating your Cover Image. For the highest quality Cover Image, press to go into fullscreen mode.

Any changes you make in the toolbox will be reflected in the preview below. When you're finished editing your Cover Image, scroll to the bottom of the page to generate it. Click here to start the tutorial if this is your first time using PS4 Cover Generator or if you just need a refresher. Be sure to check out our wiki for additional information too!
